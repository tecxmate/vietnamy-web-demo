## 1. Facebook Group

**Group Name:** `Vietnamy – Learn Vietnamese for Business & Life`  
**Target Audience:** English-speaking expats and international professionals living in or traveling to Vietnam; Western learners; Southeast Asian business people.

### Why Facebook

Vietnam has one of the highest Facebook penetration rates in Southeast Asia. Expats and international business people in Vietnam (HCMC, Hanoi) use Facebook Groups heavily for community and peer referrals.

### Setup Checklist

- [ ] Create a **public Facebook Group** (for discoverability) that converts to **private** after approval
- [ ] Cover photo: branded Vietnamy banner with tagline
- [ ] Pinned post: what the group is, rules, and link to the waitlist
- [ ] Weekly content cadence:
  - **Mon** – Word of the Week (Vietnamese business vocab)
  - **Wed** – Cultural Tip ("How to greet a Vietnamese client")
  - **Fri** – Community Discussion ("What's the hardest Vietnamese phrase you've learned this week?")
- [ ] Link the Waitlist Tally form in the group description

### Target Audience Persona

| Persona | Detail |
|---|---|
| Expat professional | Working at a foreign company HQ'd in HCMC/Hanoi |
| Business traveler | Visiting Vietnam for deals, manufacturing, sourcing |
| Lifestyle learner | Living in Vietnam, wants to integrate deeper |

## 2. LINE OpenChat

**Group Name:** `Vietnamy ベトナム語 | 越南文商務學習` (bilingual: Japanese + Traditional Chinese)  
**Target Audience:** Taiwanese and Japanese business professionals learning Vietnamese for work or relocation.

### Why LINE

LINE has ~90% penetration in Taiwan and Japan. Business communities in Taiwan and Japan almost exclusively use LINE for group communication. OpenChat allows anyone to join without sharing phone numbers — ideal for discovery.

### Setup Checklist

- [ ] Create a **LINE OpenChat** (no phone number required to join)
- [ ] Category: Language Learning / Business
- [ ] Profile image: Vietnamy logo
- [ ] Pinned message: Welcome message in **Traditional Chinese (繁體中文)** and **Japanese (日本語)**
- [ ] Content cadence (3x/week):
  - Business Vietnamese phrase of the day (Traditional Chinese translation)
  - Vietnamese cultural note relevant to Taiwanese/Japanese business practices
  - Q&A: "Ask anything about doing business in Vietnam"
- [ ] Share waitlist link in announcements

### Target Audience Persona

| Persona | Detail |
|---|---|
| Taiwanese entrepreneur | Manufacturing partner, supplier, or investor in Vietnam |
| Japanese business expat | Stationed in Vietnam for Honda, Toyota, Canon, etc. |
| Korean professional | Samsung, LG, or Hyundai employee based in Vietnam |

> **Note:** Consider creating a **KakaoTalk OpenChatroom** for Korean users as a secondary channel once you have traction.

## 3. WhatsApp Group / Channel

**Group Name:** `Vietnamy – Vietnamese for Business`  
**Target Audience:** Western (US, UK, EU, Australian) expats, NGO workers, digital nomads, and English-speaking Southeast Asian professionals.

### Why WhatsApp

WhatsApp is the default messaging app for Western expats and international travelers. It's also used heavily by Indian, Middle Eastern, and African professionals — all of whom are increasingly present in Vietnam's business scene.

### Setup Checklist

- [ ] Create a **WhatsApp Channel** (broadcast, not group) for announcements
- [ ] Separate **WhatsApp Group** for early adopters / founding members (cap at 256 initially)
- [ ] Pinned message: intro + waitlist link
- [ ] Content cadence (2x/week):
  - "Tip Tuesday" – quick Vietnamese phrase drop
  - "Feature Friday" – new app feature sneak peek or poll on what to build next
- [ ] Gate the group: only accessible via the waitlist form

### Target Audience Persona

| Persona | Detail |
|---|---|
| Western expat | Business development, consulting, startup founder in VN |
| Digital nomad | Remote worker; travels but wants to engage locally |
| NGO / Development worker | Stationed in Vietnam long-term |

## 4. Instagram

**Handle:** `@vietnamyapp` (or `@learnvietnamy`)  
**Target Audience:** Young professionals aged 22–38; lifestyle learners; aspiring travelers; people curious about Vietnam culture.

### Why Instagram

Instagram is the discovery engine for lifestyle and language-learning apps. Competitors like Duolingo, Babbel, and Preply all have strong Instagram presences. It's also strong in the Taiwanese and Japanese market.

### Content Strategy

| Format | Frequency | Content |
|---|---|---|
| **Reels** | 3x/week | 30-60s Vietnamese phrase videos ("How to order coffee like a local") |
| **Carousels** | 2x/week | "5 Vietnamese words every business traveler needs", tone guides |
| **Stories** | Daily | Polls, Q&A, behind-the-scenes, user shoutouts |
| **Feed Posts** | 3x/week | Cultural photos, quotes, app screenshots |

### Visual Identity

- Dark, premium aesthetic (consistent with the app's dark theme)
- Vietnamese cultural motifs (ao dai, coffee culture, street food, architecture)
- Text overlays in clean modern fonts (match the app's design language)
- Hashtags: `#learnvietnamese` `#vietnamexpat` `#languagelearning` `#businessvietnamese` `#vietnamtravel`

### Content Pillars

1. **Learn** – Daily vocabulary, phrases, grammar tips
2. **Culture** – Business etiquette, food, festivals, lifestyle
3. **Community** – User stories, testimonials, founding member spotlights
4. **App** – Feature reveals, behind-the-scenes development

## 5. Threads (Meta)

**Handle:** `@vietnamyapp` (same as Instagram, linked account)  
**Target Audience:** Tech-savvy early adopters, language learning enthusiasts, startup community, expat thought leaders.

### Why Threads

Threads integrates with Instagram's audience but has a more conversational/Twitter-like tone. It's gaining traction in the startup and product community — good for reaching early adopters and generating organic press/word-of-mouth.

### Content Strategy

- Low-production, high-authenticity: behind the scenes of building the app
- "Building in public" posts: "We just shipped X, here's what we learned"
- Engagement bait: "What's the hardest part of learning Vietnamese? 👇"
- Repost user wins and community highlights from other platforms
- Announce new features and product updates conversationally

### Posting Cadence

- 1–2 short posts/day
- Reply to comments and engage with the language learning / expat community
- Cross-post Instagram Reels to Threads for max reach
